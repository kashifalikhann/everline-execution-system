## PORTAL 1: BIDNET DIRECT (Morris County Cooperative)
**Priority: DO THIS FIRST — covers multiple entities at once**

- **URL:** https://www.bidnetdirect.com/new-jersey
- **Cost:** Free one-time vendor access (or $49.95 for premium)
- **Covers:** All Morris County Cooperative Pricing Council members
- **Confirmed members:** Denville, Randolph, Lincoln Park + others

**STEP-BY-STEP:**
1. Go to https://www.bidnetdirect.com/new-jersey
2. Click "Register" or "Vendor Registration"
3. Fill in company details:
   - Company Name: Everline Coatings & Services
   - Address: [Client's NJ address]
   - Phone: [Client's phone]
   - Email: [Client's email]
   - NJ Business Registration #: [BRC number]
   - PWCR #: [PWCR number]
4. Select ALL relevant commodity categories:
   - Pavement Markings / Line Striping
   - Parking Lot Maintenance
   - Road Maintenance / Repair
   - Sealcoating
   - Crack Filling
   - Pothole Repair
   - Playground Equipment / Surfacing
   - Athletic Field Markings
   - Traffic Control / Safety
   - Asphalt Services
   - General Construction / Public Works
5. Upload documents:
   - BRC Certificate
   - PWCR Certificate
   - MBE Certificate
   - Certificate of Insurance
   - W-9
6. Submit and save confirmation number
7. Check email for activation link — click it
8. Set up bid notification alerts for Morris County, NJ

## PORTAL 7: NJSTART (NJ State Procurement)
**State-level procurement portal**

- **URL:** https://www.njstart.gov
- **Cost:** Free to register

**STEP-BY-STEP:**
1. Go to https://www.njstart.gov
2. Click "Vendor Registration"
3. Complete profile with BRC #, PWCR #, MBE #
4. Select NIGP commodity codes:
   - 395-40: Pavement Markings
   - 445-20: Parking Lot Equipment
   - 550-60: Resurfacing/Resealing
   - 600-20: Maintenance - Roadways
   - 910-63: Street/Highway Maintenance
   - 968-15: Athletic/Sports Field Marking
5. Upload all certificates
6. Wait for approval (typically 5-10 business days)

**STATUS:** ☐ NOT STARTED  ☐ IN PROGRESS  ☐ COMPLETE

## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **MCCPC is the warmest opportunity.** Client is already "in process." Once MCCPC is active, automatically visible to all members via BidNet.
2. **Apply to ALL cooperatives in parallel.** Don't wait for one to approve before starting the next.
3. **Use same 6 documents for every portal:** BRC, PWCR, MBE, Insurance, W-9, Capabilities.
4. **NIGP Codes matter.** Wrong categories = never see relevant bids. Select broadly.
5. **Set up email alerts** on every portal. You want to know the instant a relevant bid drops.
6. **Re-check portals weekly.** New entities join cooperatives all the time.
