## THE 3-LANE STRATEGY

We don't treat every entity the same. Each gets the approach that converts best:

### Lane A — Portal Self-Service (10 entities)
Fill out registration forms online. No calls needed. Done in hours.

### Lane B — Email Direct (40 schools)
Send vendor package to verified BA emails. Follow up in 3 days. Call only if no reply after 2nd follow-up.

### Lane C — Call + Email (23 municipalities)
Call the named QPA/Clerk directly. Send email same day with all 6 documents.
