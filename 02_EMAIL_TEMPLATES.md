## EMAIL SENDING RULES

1. **Always attach all 6 documents.** Every time. No exceptions.
2. **Use the BA's first name.** Not "Dear Sir/Madam."
3. **Send between 8:30-10:00 AM.** Tuesday through Thursday. Avoid Monday and Friday.
4. **Log every send** in the master tracker immediately.
5. **Set a reminder** for Day 3 follow-up on every email sent.
6. **If they reply with a question**, respond within 4 hours. Speed wins.
7. **If they say "we don't need anyone"**, respond: "Understood. Can I leave our docs on file in case something comes up?" This keeps the door open.
