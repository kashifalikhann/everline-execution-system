## PART 5: LEAD SCORING AND SEGMENTATION

### Cold Lead (downloaded entity list, no call booked):
- Gets Email Sequence (5 emails over 10 days)
- After sequence: monthly newsletter with updates
- Re-target with LinkedIn ads after 30 days

### Warm Lead (downloaded + clicked pricing):
- Gets Email Sequence + special offer email
- Gets LinkedIn connection request from Kashif
- Gets direct phone call from us

### Hot Lead (booked a call):
- Gets pre-call email with agenda
- Gets the call (15 min close or diagnose)
- Gets post-call follow-up with proposal
- Gets 3 follow-up emails if no response

### Scoring:
| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Visited landing page | 1 |
| Downloaded entity list | 5 |
| Clicked pricing | 10 |
| Clicked "book a call" | 15 |
| Booked call | 25 |
| Showed up to call | 30 |
| Asked about pricing | 40 |

**Score 0-5:** Cold → Nurture sequence
**Score 6-15:** Warm → Direct outreach
**Score 16-30:** Hot → Priority follow-up
**Score 31+:** Closing → Proposal + close

## PART 8: MOBILE-FIRST DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Why Mobile-First:
- Contractors are on job sites, not in offices
- They browse on phones during downtime
- Government forms are often filled on mobile
- Speed matters more than beauty

### Requirements:
| Element | Desktop | Mobile |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Page load | <2 seconds | <1.5 seconds |
| Hero headline | 8-10 words | 6-8 words |
| CTA button | 48px height | 56px height (thumb-friendly) |
| Form fields | 2 (email + company) | 1 (email only) |
| Sections | 8 sections | 5 sections (combine some) |
| Images | Full size | Compressed, lazy-loaded |
| Navigation | Not needed | Not needed (single page) |

### Mobile-Specific Rules:
- NO pop-ups (Google penalizes, mobile users hate them)
- Click-to-call phone number in header
- Sticky CTA button at bottom of screen
- Form above the fold (no scrolling to sign up)
- Font size minimum 16px (no zooming required)
